we are Katherine BUTCHER & Carlos A. CORREIA
studio [at] butchercorreia.com

Their artistic practice critically engages with the intricate tapestry of local and global political and social landscapes, unraveling the structures shaping contemporary society. Through performance, immersive environments, and physical objects, their work serves as both articulation and critique, addressing themes of labor, hierarchy, and social norms. This collaboration is an open-ended dialogue, continuously unfolding through critical engagement and creative experimentation. Their distinct methodologies intersect within this framework, fostering a dynamic space for interrogation, expansion, and reimagining of artistic practice. Rather than a finite endeavor, it remains an evolving exploration of systemic structures and their implications.
